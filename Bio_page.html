About Me

I'm a junior majoring in Computer Systems with a passion for cybersecurity and web development. After participating in programs like Upperline Code and completing a cybersecurity certification, I’ve been driven to learn how technology can be used to protect people—especially in communities like mine. Whether I’m coding a website or exploring new digital tools, I’m always looking for ways to make tech more accessible and meaningful.

Outside of tech, I have a deep love for creativity and leadership. I’ve worked as a project manager for a fashion company and served as a student ambassador, where I developed skills in organization, communication, and team-building. Whether I’m managing a project or mentoring others, I enjoy creating environments where people can thrive and feel supported.
